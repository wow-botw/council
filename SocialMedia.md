# 🐺 **SOCIAL MEDIA & RECORDING AGREEMENT [english]**

*Status: 24.04.2026*

In some of our Discord channels, we create content for Twitch, TikTok, Instagram, and possibly other platforms. To ensure transparency and fairness for everyone, the following applies:

---

## 🎥 **What can be recorded?**

By joining a “Recording” channel, the following content may be recorded and published:

* 🎤 Your voice (voice chat)
* 🎮 Your in-game name and/or Discord name
* 💬 Conversations and interactions in the channel
* 🎬 Gameplay associated with you

This content may be edited (e.g., cut/trimmed) and publicly published on our various social media channels.

---

## ✅ **Your consent**

By joining a Recording channel, you confirm that:

* You agree to the recording and publication of the content listed above
* You understand that content published on the internet is publicly accessible
* You participate voluntarily

---

## 🔄 **Your control**

* Participation is always voluntary
* You can leave Recording channels at any time
* Alternatively, “Safe” channels without recording are available
* You can withdraw your consent at any time with future effect

  * However, already published content cannot be removed from the internet

👉 In case of withdrawal, please contact the Council

---

## ⚠️ **Important notes**

* Please do not share sensitive or private information in Recording channels
* Maintain respectful behavior – you represent the pack 🐺
* Participation is intended only for those aware of the implications of public content

---

## 📌 **Consent**

By reacting with 👍 to this message, you apply for the “Voice of the Pack” role on our Discord. Once you receive this role, you will see the voice channels where recording takes place and may join them.

# 🐺 **Etiquette & Vision – Recording Channels**

Our Recording channels are not just for clips – they represent **who we are as a guild to the outside world**.

---

## 🧠 **Our Vision**

We want to create content that shows:

* calm, structured gameplay
* respectful interaction
* shared growth instead of ego

👉 No flaming, no shouting – just what defines our pack.

---

## 🎤 **Etiquette in Recording Channels**

* Remember: everything can be recorded and published
* Speak clearly, calmly, and respectfully
* No private info, real names, or sensitive topics in voice
* Humor is welcome – but not at the expense of others
* If someone says “please don’t clip” → respect it

---

## 🐺 **Important**

You are not only representing yourself here, but the entire pack.
How we play internally is how we want to be seen externally.

**Together we hunt – together we grow.**
