By joining this server, you agree to the **Discord Community Guidelines**. In addition, the following rules apply:

1. **Be a Gentleman (or gentlewoman, or anything in between):**  
   This is an international community of gamers from different backgrounds, countries, ethnicities, and walks of life. We welcome everyone.  
   :x: Disrespect, discrimination, or toxic behavior will not be tolerated.

2. **Don't Be the Reason Someone Logs Off:**  
   :white_check_mark: Be decent. Don’t ruin the fun for others. You're here to play and chill — not to start fires.

3. **Game Rules Apply:**  
   :video_game: Each game we support may have its own set of rules.  
   - If a game bans mods, *we* ban them too.  
   - **No cheats, hacks, exploits, or spawn-blocking. Ever.**

4. **About Our Self-Hosted Servers:**  
   :tools: We're not full-time hosts. This is a hobby.  
   - No guarantees.  
   - No official support.  
   - No SLAs.  
   :speech_balloon: Feedback is welcome — rudeness is not.

5. **Language Rules:**  
   :earth_africa: The **official server language is English.**  
   - Use English in all channels **unless** marked with `-DE`.  
   - Some channels may be mostly German — adapt respectfully.

6. **Breaking the Rules:**  
   :no_entry_sign: Toxicity, rule-breaking, or being an asshole can result in a **ban from both Discord and our game servers**.  
   This is your first — and only — polite warning.
